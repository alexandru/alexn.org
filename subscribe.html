RSS Web Feed

An alternative to the email newsletter is following the website's RSS feed. You can do this with various applications, that run both locally or online, out of which the recommended ones would be:

  • Newsblur (web, open-source)
  • Miniflux (web, open-source, self-hosted)
  • Feedly (web)
  • Reeder (MacOS, iOS)
