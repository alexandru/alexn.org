Alexandru Nedelcu

I'm a Software Developer, currently living in Bucharest, Romania.

Status: Not looking for work / taken and perfectly happy!

I'm a full stack developer capable of handling everything, from back-end development and administering systems, to front-end design and user experience. I'm experienced in a wide range of cutting-edge technologies and problem domains, emphasizing on better algorithms, quality and user experience. Since I worked as a consultant, I've had the pleasure of talking to real people, understanding and solving real world problems.
